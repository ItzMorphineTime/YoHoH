- Stream the game, post gameplay videos, clips, and screenshots.
- Monetize those videos/streams via platform ads, donations, subs, memberships, and sponsorships
  (as long as you don’t claim we endorse you).
- Share your fan art non-commercially (social media, portfolio, etc.).

Please do:

- Sell fan art/merch/prints/NFTs/paid downloads.
